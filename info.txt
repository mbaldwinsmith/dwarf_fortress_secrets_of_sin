[STEAM_TITLE:Seven Secrets of Sin]
[STEAM_DESCRIPTION:Seven Secrets of Sin
A psychological catastrophe in every fortress.

What is this mod?
Seven Secrets of Sin introduces seven forbidden secrets based on the Seven Deadly Sins. These aren't monsters or explosions, they are ideas that warp emotion, personality, and society itself.

Each secret can be learned through books, taught by scholars, and leaves a permanent mark on its bearer. Once a secret is known, its aura radiates emotional influence on nearby intelligent creatures, reshaping fortress life in subtle, dramatic, and FUN ways.

Features

Seven Learnable Secrets. Each sin is a unique secret with a narrative identity (Order of Pride, Order of Greed, and so on), a permanent personality change when learned, and an aura that radiates emotional effects to nearby intelligent beings.

Worldgen Integration. Secrets are actual secret-type interactions. They appear in Legends mode, can be learned, researched, and recorded, and propagate through written content and teaching.

Gameplay Impact. This mod is about mood, not mass slaughter.

Compatibility and Requirements. Raw-only mod, no external tools required. Works with vanilla secrets and worldgen. Written content (books) encouraged for fullest experience.

Contribute. Github repository at github.com/mbaldwinsmith/dwarf_fortress_secrets_of_sin]
