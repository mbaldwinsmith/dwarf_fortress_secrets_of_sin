[NAME:Seven Secrets of Sin]
[DESCRIPTION:The Seven Deadly Sins are not merely vices, but hidden teachings, whispered rites, and lingering corruptions of the soul.

This mod introduces seven supernatural Secrets, each aligned with one of the Seven Deadly Sins. Those who learn a Secret may perform a Rite, marking themselves with a Sin-Curse that reshapes their personality and grants a powerful emotional aura.

Each aura projects temptation, unrest, or distortion through line-of-sight interactions, subtly influencing the thoughts and emotions of nearby intelligent creatures. Shame, envy, wrath, arousal, despair, and guilt ripple outward, leaving social and psychological consequences rather than simple physical harm.

Secrets are anchored to divine spheres during world generation, allowing sinful cults, corrupted priests, and tragic historical figures to emerge organically.

This is a raw-only mod. Generate a new world to ensure proper secret and sphere integration. Balance feedback and emergent stories are very welcome.]

[i]A psychological catastrophe in every fortress.[/i]

[h2]What is this mod?[/h2]
Seven Secrets of Sin introduces seven forbidden secrets based on the Seven Deadly Sins. These aren’t monsters or explosions — they are ideas that warp emotion, personality, and society itself.

Each secret can be learned through books, taught by scholars, and leaves a permanent mark on its bearer. Once a secret is known, its aura radiates emotional influence on nearby intelligent creatures, reshaping fortress life in subtle, dramatic, and FUN ways.

[h2]Features[/h2]

[b]📚 Seven Learnable Secrets[/b]
Each sin is a unique secret with:
[list]
[*][b]A narrative identity[/b] — “Order of Pride,” “Order of Greed,” etc.
[*][b]Permanent personality change[/b] when learned
[*][b]An aura that radiates emotional effects[/b] to nearby intelligent beings
[/list]

[b]📖 Worldgen Integration[/b]
Secrets are actual [I_SOURCE:SECRET] interactions. They:
[list]
[*]Appear in Legends mode
[*]Can be learned, researched, and recorded
[*]Propagate through written content and teaching
[/list]

[h2]Gameplay Impact[/h2]

[i]This mod is about mood, not mass slaughter.[/i]

[h2]Compatibility & Requirements[/h2]
✔ Raw-only mod — no external tools required
✔ Works with vanilla secrets and worldgen
✔ Written content (books) encouraged for fullest experience]
